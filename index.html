Who we are

Priority America is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization focused on helping veterans translate military experience into marketable employment skills in the field of information technology.

What We Do.

Military values such as integrity, loyalty, team work, accountability, and leadership are some of the most desired employee traits. Our goal is to provide an environment that enables veterans to transform their experience in to sustainable long-term career skills by blending together technical knowledge with military experience and mission focus.

The Federal Government and Government related technology industries have a significant preference to hire veterans as directed by Congress. However, existing transition programs fall short in enabling veterans to start a new career; instead they focus on basic benefit education and vocational training. Priority America aims to fill this gap.

Priority America is a group of experienced and motivated Federal hiring managers and industry experts focused on the career enablement of veterans. Our program is composed of the following five areas:

Transition Services

Assistance in mapping skills learned in the military to language understood by Federal and non-military hiring organizations. Such as military to civilian resume alignment.

Career Roadmaps

Step-by-step career roadmaps for some of the most sought after technology jobs today. Such as coding, cloud engineering, and cyber security.

Priority Labs

Fully functioning consultancy working on delivering real projects, and a real-life environment for veterans to practice what they have learned and earn direct work experience. It is also a safe transition space where veterans can develop the soft skills required to be successful in a professional environment.

Career Network

Connect with hiring authorities. Events focused on connecting veterans directly with hiring managers to understand what it takes to get hired. Priority America has a large network of experts that are available provide mentorship throughout career transition and growth.

Training & Education

Recommended training and educational partners. We review and partner with reputable institutions and groups to maximize the benefits of the GI bill.
